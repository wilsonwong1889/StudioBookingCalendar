Arrival

What to do before you walk in

A few small steps make the session feel smoother once you reach the studio.

1 Open the map first

Check the route before you leave so you are not searching for the building at the last minute.

2 Bring what you need for the session

Drives, files, instruments, references, and any special gear should be ready before arrival.

3 Build in buffer time

Arriving a little early gives you time to park, unload, and settle in without rushing setup.
