FAQ

Common booking questions, answered clearly.

Use this page to answer the questions guests usually ask before they commit to a room, a staff add-on, or a session date.

Bookings

Before you reserve

1 How long is each booking?

Bookings can be created from 1 hour up to the maximum duration configured for that room, using whole-hour steps only.

2 Can I add staff support?

Yes. If a room has staff profiles attached, you can add them during booking and see the price before completing the session.

3 Can I book more than one session?

The current setup is optimized for one session per day per account, which helps prevent accidental overbooking and keeps the schedule fair.

Studio use

Arrival and room expectations

4 When should I arrive?

Plan to arrive 10 to 15 minutes early so your group can check in, unload, and be ready when the booked session begins.

5 Can I bring guests?

Yes, but stay within the room capacity shown on the room page so the session remains comfortable and safe.

6 What should I bring?

Bring project files, drives, reference tracks, and any specialty equipment you need. The room pages should outline what each space is best suited for.
