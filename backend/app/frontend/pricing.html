Booking policies & cancellation

Cancellation policy

  • Cancel 24+ hours before booking: deposit refunded.
  • Cancel less than 24 hours before: deposit forfeited.
  • No-show: deposit forfeited.
  • Late arrival or early departure: full booked time is still charged.
