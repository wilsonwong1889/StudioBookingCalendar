About

What this studio is for

This studio is designed for recording sessions, podcast capture, production shoots, strategy meetings, content creation, and client-facing creative work.

The booking platform is meant to keep the process direct: browse the room, pick the time, add any staff support you need, and arrive with a clear understanding of the space.

House rules

What guests should expect on site

1 Respect start times

Choose enough time for setup, the session itself, and wrap so the room can turn over cleanly.

2 Stay within room capacity

Capacity exists to keep the room comfortable, safe, and workable for everyone in the session.

3 Leave the room ready for turnover

Clear personal items, gear, and trash before the session ends so the next booking starts cleanly.
